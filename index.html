Group Chat Fantasy League

2017 - 2018 Season

Regular Season

1. Each person will draft their team, and players selected will not change throughout the entire season. (set-and-forget)

2. The winning order will determine amount of money added to the playoff bracket buy-in.

  • First Place – Free
  • Second Place - $1
  • Third Place - $2
  • Fourth Place - $3
  • Fifth Place – $4
  • Sixth Place - $5

** Tie breakers will be settled in a GLHF Best-Of-7 Rock paper scissors administered in the presence of another league member.

3. Each year, the amount required for each place will by the original amount multiplied by the number of years of ongoing.

  • Example: 5th year
      • Sixth Place - $5 * 5 years = $25
  • (first place will always be free)
Post Season

1. There will be incremental earnings for the winners

  • First place winnings – 50% (rounded up to the nearest dollar)
  • Second place winnings – 30% (rounded up to the nearest dollar)
  • Third place winnings – 20% (rounded up to the nearest dollar)
  • Fourth place winnings – nothing
  • Fifth place winnings – nothing
  • Sixth place winnings – nothing

2. The order of winning will determine the following years draft order.

  • Sixth place – number 1 pick in the draft
  • Fifth place – number 2 pick in the draft
  • Fourth place – number 3 pick in the draft
  • Third place – number 4 pick in the draft
  • Second place – number 5 pick in the draft
  • First place – number 6 pick in the draft

** Tie breakers will be settled in a GLHF Best-Of-7 Rock paper scissors administered in the presence of another league member
